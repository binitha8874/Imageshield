Core capabilities

Every registered image receives a unique cryptographic licence key tied to your Gmail and phone number for ironclad ownership proof.

Three Visibility Modes

Choose Public (open attribution), Private (owner only), or Restricted (approved parties only). Change anytime.

Internet-Wide Scanning

Our crawler detects unauthorised use of your images across social media, websites, and stock platforms automatically.

📱

2FA Verification

Analytics Dashboard

Track views, licence requests, detected violations, and revenue from licensing all in one clean dashboard.

DMCA Takedown Generator

How it works

Three steps to protect your image

1️⃣

Register

Enter your Gmail and phone number. Verify with OTP. Upload your image and choose a visibility setting.

2️⃣

Lock

Receive your unique 32-character licence key. Your image is fingerprinted and registered in our global database.

3️⃣

Monitor

Dashboard alerts you when your image is found online. Share the key with approved parties or issue takedowns.
